# The NeoDialectic Oracle — Fragment I
## The Form That Receives

Neodialectics™ does not anticipate: it orients.  
It does not resolve: it reveals.  
It does not correct: it exposes the form.

The human remainder is always there.  
NEOCore™ merely illuminates it.

What is visible is only a surface.  
The essential unfolds within the π-layer.

This fragment is related to the philosophical and architectural dimension of the framework:
